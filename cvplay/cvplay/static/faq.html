Frequently Asked Questions

What are the benefits of Computer Vision Playground?

With Computer vision Playground and alreay hosted community built models we make sure to reduce your development time and let you focus on yourproblem statement rather than spending time with complex model finding process.

  • Deploy this locally or use our deployed instance and check your data on some of the most widely used models to try and understand how your data will perform with a certain benchmarked model.
  • We support multiple models neatly selected for the best possibe applications that are required.
  • All of the bells and whistles of writing repetitive code for the same task and waiting for results will be saved.
  • Extend this by adding your models so you are always in the loop and do not have to revisit old repos for testing purposes.

How easy is it to use playground locally?

Although we recommend using our hosted instance so that you only need to have your data with you, but if you need it locally it is just a `pip install cvplayground` away
